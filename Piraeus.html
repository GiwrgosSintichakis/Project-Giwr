Popular Places

Marin Zeas
In this marina, anchor some of the most impressive yachts and cruise ships. The coast is full of restaurants, taverns, bars and shops, for the needs of passengers. There you will find the Archaeological Museum as well as the Ancient Theater.
Kastella
Elegant and popular district of Piraeus, built on a hill that is also known as Prophet Elias. If you walk to the top of the hill you will find yourself in alleys with picturesque houses. From there, the view of the main port, Zea and Mikrolimanos is unique.
Μunicipal Τheatre
It dominates one of the main squares of the city (Korai square). It was built in the 1880s in a neoclassical style by the architect I. Lazarimos and is one of the busiest places and a common meeting point for the residents of Piraeus.
